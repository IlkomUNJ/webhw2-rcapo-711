About Callys Bakery

Our Story

Callys Bakery started in 2015 as a small home bakery with a passion for creating the most delicious brownies and cookies. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a beloved local business.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients - real butter, premium chocolate, and organic eggs - to create treats that are not only delicious but made with love and care.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with a wide variety of brownies, cookies, and other baked goods, all made fresh daily.

Award Winning

We source our ingredients locally and use no artificial additives.

Made with Love
